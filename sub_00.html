We will pave the way for the Korean economy to take its next great leap on the global stage.



This was the promise made to the people when the FKI announced its new beginning last year. Reflecting on our founding principle from 1961, “Serving the Nation Through Business,” we committed ourselves to becoming a global think tank that communicates with the public as a trusted partner.



The birth of 다함께, ALL TOGETHER is a part of fulfilling that promise. We aim to share with you the essential knowledge and wisdom necessary in this day and age. The challenging path toward the future of the Korean economy cannot be taken by us alone. It requires the joint effort of the public, the government, and business leaders. That’s why we named this magazine 다함께, ALL TOGETHER. We will listen to your diverse voices and pave the way for shared success.

다함께, ALL TOGETHER is a premium economic magazine. It covers timely domestic and international economic issues, legislative trends, and features interviews and success stories of renowned figures. Like a microscope, we will examine issues with high precision, and like a telescope, we will offer a broader perspective down the road and telescope.



There is also a reason why we chose a print publication in an era dominated by online content. Unlike digital media, which often fragments meaning into discrete pieces and is constrained by personal data boundaries, we believe in the power of print media to facilitate free communication that can be passed from hand to hand and to present the entire picture at a glance.



We are on the brink of a massive wave of change. As we navigate the vast ocean of the global economy, 다함께, ALL TOGETHER will be your compass. Bolstered by your support and engagement, FKI will continue to embrace challenges and drive innovation, leading the Korean economy in taking its next great leap.



We look forward to your continued interest and support.


Thank you.
